Add macOS menu-bar tray icon with quick-capture popover (Phase 3.2).

A Compass compass-needle icon lives permanently in the macOS menu bar. Left-clicking the icon (or pressing Cmd+Shift+Space from any app) opens a frameless 360×80 popover directly below the tray icon. Type a task title and press Enter to add it to today's daily checklist; press Esc or click away to dismiss. The shortcut chord can be changed by writing `quickCaptureShortcut` to the `app_settings` table.
